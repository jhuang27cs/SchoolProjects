I always, always, ALWAYS go here since it's a block away from my boyfriend's apartment. It's a small place, but the yogurt has improved greatly from when it first opened. The service is always friendly and willing to give free samples. 8) 

My personal favorites are original tart (of course ;) ), lychee, and taro. Unfortunately they don't rotate the flavors a lot so it's mostly the same thing everytime. :(

Wide variety of toppings, and they even put witty names on the handle.

only 30 cents/ounce when you show your student ID!
