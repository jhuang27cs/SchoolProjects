Incredibly knowledgeable cheese store with hundreds of varieties.  They also have pretty much every accompaniment you could imagine to pair with cheese: crackers, salamis, jams, etc.  Its just the store that Princeton needed to accommodate cheese lovers and people looking for a unique solution for their next party.  They also do gift trays and some sandwiches to go.  

Finally they have this great card catalog system that allows you to track your cheese purchases.  Now you never have to wonder what you bought or what you've tried.  They do it for you!
