While the staff is friendly and helpful, the store doesn't have half the items that the rest of the chain does in Manhattan. No Redken products, limited in other areas as well.

There's always a long line to check out, they never seem to  have enough cashiers and when they have a sale on Kashi cereal, if you're not there the first day, you are out of luck.

Still, it's very clean inside and their printer ink and Starbucks ground coffee  is cheap
