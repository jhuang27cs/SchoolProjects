One word: overrated. No, two words: overrated, and overpriced.  

Fine, we can still call it a Harvard Institution if you want, but if you are craving burgers (like I do most days) a better bet is Miracle of Science.

Fries were nothing to write home about either.  Chips seemed like were Frito-Lays.  Mushrooms were rubbery and odd-tasting, and I'm a mushroom lover too.

And to think how much worse my review would be if I actually had to wait in a line.
